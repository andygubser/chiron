# What is data science?
Data science is the discipline of making data useful, and it's an umbrella term for three subdisciplines:


- Analytics
    + Descriptive Analytics or Data Mining
    + Get inspired

- Machine learning
    + Make recipe

- Statistics 
    + (or Statistical Inference)
    + Decide wisely
